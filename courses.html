Choose the course that’s right for you.

Our instructor-led courses have weekly live, online sessions with expert instructors supplemented by learning materials in the form of an Online Music Book - OM Book(tm) with audio/video lessons, theory, notations, lyrics. Each session is 45 minutes in duration with intimate batch sizes of 1 to 3 students. Choose your own timings for your session and get started right away - all you need in a laptop/computer, an internet and your headphones.
